About

Have a conversation or leave an unguided audio journal entry.

You have the choice of either chatting with our trained AI bot that asks you questions for guidance or freely talking about anything of your choice. We analyze your audio entries and replies to our AI bot to determine your overall mood for each day.

Keep track of your moods and key words.

This is your personalized page to see how your mood varied or stayed the same throughout each day or month. We also display your frequently used keywords that affect your moods.

Go back to your old entries.

We convert your audio journal entries to text so you can conveniently read them in the future. Click on any day in the calendar to see your journal entry for that day.

Listen to the perfect playlists to match the ambiance.

After you finish your entry for the day, you're provided with a Spotify playlist to complement your mood. Studies have shown that regardless of the genre or type of music, listening to music in general releases dopamine. In addition, the app develops a list of activities geared towards improving your mood, informed by data collected from your journal.
